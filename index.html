Acer

Our founder, Alina Nedelcu, saw the ineffectiveness of the Romanian public school system and private institutions in addressing the needs of children with learning disabilities. In response, she launched Acer in 2019, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering children with autism and other educational challenges. At Acer, we believe in the power of inclusion, understanding, and community. Our holistic approach emphasizes early intervention, education, and social development, fostering environments where children feel valued and supported. We work closely with parents, educators, and specialists to ensure each child receives the personalized care they need to succeed, both at home and in school.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to create a world where every child on the autism spectrum has the opportunity to thrive, learn, and grow. We offer a range of tailored programs, resources, and therapies designed to meet the unique needs of each child, helping them build the skills and confidence necessary to navigate life’s challenges.
